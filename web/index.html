Cellular TTL Manager

Bypass Hotspot Data Limits for Verizon and T-Mobile

A cross-platform utility built for the "Embedded Witness." This application provides a seamless way to modify your system's networking parameters, allowing you to bypass carrier-imposed data restrictions and hotspots caps.

What this means: This tool changes your computer’s "TTL" setting so that your internet traffic looks like it is coming directly from your phone rather than a tethered hotspot. Your data usage will register against your regular phone plan, not your hotspot limit.

Key Features:

  • One-Click TTL Modification (65 for Verizon, 64 for T-Mobile).
  • Full IPv4 and IPv6 support for modern cellular networks.
  • Cross-platform: Works on Windows, macOS, and Linux.
  • Built-in connection tester to verify your bypass is active.
