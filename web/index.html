Hotspot Unlock

Turn your limited Hotspot into an Infinite Phone Plan

Hotspot Unlock effectively transforms your computer into a cell phone in the eyes of your carrier. By masking your system's network signature, it allows you to draw from your **primary mobile data pool** instead of a restricted, capped hotspot bucket.

The Vision: Your computer is now a phone. By synchronizing your TTL identity, this tool allows your laptop to use your phone's unlimited data plan, effectively turning a limited hotspot into an infinite phone plan experience.

Key Features:

  • One-Click Hotspot Unlocking (65 for Verizon, 64 for T-Mobile).
  • Full IPv4 and IPv6 support for modern cellular networks.
  • Cross-platform: Works on Windows, macOS, and Linux.
  • Built-in connection tester to verify your unlock is active.
