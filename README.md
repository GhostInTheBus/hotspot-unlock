# Hotspot Unlock 🚀
**The Infinite Phone Plan Experience**

Hotspot Unlock is a professional-grade, cross-platform utility designed to transform your computer into a cell phone in the eyes of your carrier. By masking your system's network TTL signature, it allows you to draw from your **primary mobile data plan** instead of a restricted hotspot bucket.

## 🌟 Vision
Do you have an unlimited phone plan but a limited hotspot plan? This utility makes your hotspot plan unlimited like your phone. It effectively turns a limited connection into an infinite phone plan experience by synchronizing your system's identity with your mobile device.

## ✨ Key Features
- **One-Click Unlock:** Optimized profiles for **Verizon / Visible (65)** and **T-Mobile / Metro (64)**.
- **Persistence Engine:**
  - **macOS:** Auto-creates a `LaunchDaemon` (`com.hotspotunlock.ttl`) to keep the bypass active after reboots.
  - **Windows:** Performs a "Triple-Tap" (netsh default + netsh current + Registry) for bulletproof persistence.
- **Dual-Stack Support:** Full coverage for both **IPv4 and IPv6** network stacks.
- **Auto-Elevation:** Native system integration prompts for administrative privileges immediately on startup.
- **Live Verification:** Built-in connection tester to verify the unlock status in real-time.
- **Pro Dark UI:** Modern, high-contrast interface designed for ease of use.

## 🚀 Quick Start
1. **Download:** Grab the latest release for [Mac or Windows](https://github.com/GhostInTheBus/hotspot-unlock/releases).
2. **Authorize:** Open the app and provide your system password when prompted.
3. **Select Carrier:** Choose your provider from the dropdown.
4. **Unlock:** Click **"Unlock Infinite Plan"**.
5. **Verify:** Use the **"Test Connection"** button to confirm success.

> **⚠️ Note for Mac Users:** 
> As an independent, unsigned utility, macOS may flag this. To open: **Right-click** the icon and select **Open**.
